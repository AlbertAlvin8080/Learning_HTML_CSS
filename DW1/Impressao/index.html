Key Terms

Burn-in Period:
The first M iterations in a Markov chain.
Proposal Distribution:
sometimes called an instrumental distribution or a candidate- generating, the proposal distribution may be chosen arbitrarily, although there may be efficiency advantages to one form over another in some applications. The proposal distribution satisfies the key condition for density functions.
